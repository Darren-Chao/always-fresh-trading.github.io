Operation Center

Our Cooling Hub & Climate Management

Our operations revolve around our cooling terminal in Downtown Los Angeles. Spanning thousands of square feet, this facility processes inbound shipments from California's primary farming valleys. We personally inspect, sort, and cross-dock every load for local distribution.

Cold Storage Room

Maintained at a constant 32°F - 36°F. Essential for preservation of leafy greens, broccoli, strawberries, and crisp celery.

Temperate Citrus Zone

Kept at a steady 45°F - 50°F with controlled relative humidity. Customized environment optimized for oranges, bell peppers, and root tubers.

Tropical Vaults

Strict climate management set at 54°F - 58°F to prevent chilling injury and control ripening curves of mangoes and warm-weather melons.

Wet-Pack Conditioning

Re-icing bays and humidified sorting zones equipped to process heavy commercial cilantro, leafy parsley, and green onion bunched crates.
